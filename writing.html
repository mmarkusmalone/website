Fiction: The Arrow

"The Arrow," is fictional piece is centered on a boy who goes hunting alone for the first time.

"Smile," is another fictional piece that explores the lingering effects of an old relationship.

In "Creak," the acts of intrusion and numbing unfold in a mini-drama between an intruder and oblivious girl.
